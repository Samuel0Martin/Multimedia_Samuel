Animation Page
Script:

This animation is about the main character who is an explorer, and is in search of a legendary sword.
He locates a dungeon where he thinks the sword is located and enters.
As he wanders around the labyrinth like dungeon he finds a coin which he picks up and takes.
After finding his way to a room far on the left he enters it hoping to find the sword.
He instead runs into a snake which awakens and attacks.
The explorer selects the option to attack the snake after moving to run.

He defeats the snake and leaves the room in a hurry.
He runs to a room at the top and looks around finding a treasure chest.
He walks up to it and opens it, finding the legendary sword.
He takes the sword and leaves the dungeon.
When he is out of the dungeon he proudly holds up the sword above his head.
He then leaves the area and returns home.


The End
