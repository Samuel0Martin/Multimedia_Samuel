Script:

Scene 1

The main character is an explorer searching for the legendary sword.

When he is thinking about the sword, he comes across a dungeon and descides to enter

Scene 2

After he enters the dungeon he moves around the labyrith like structure and finds a coin.

He picks up the coin and continues on his exploration and enters the room to the left.

Scene 3

He enters the left room to find a snake that awakens and attacks.

Scene 4

The battle with the snake begins.

The Explorer moves the cursor down towards the Run action.

However he changes to the Attack action and selects it.

He then attacks the snake a defeats it.

Scene 5

After defeating the snake the Explorer leaves the room and runs to the top room on the main floor.

Scene 6

The Explorer enters the top room and looks to the right sees nothing then looks to the right and discovers a treasure chest.

He walks up to the chest and opens it, revealing the legendary sword inside.

He recieves the sword and leaves the room.

Scene 7

The Explorer then makes his way back to the dungeon enterance and leaves.

Once he has left the dungeon he makes his way out of the area.

He holds up the sword and observes it.

He then leaves the area.

The End
