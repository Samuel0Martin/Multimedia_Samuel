Animation Page
Research:

I based my animation off a top down RPG style game.

The games that I took inspiration from were


The Legend of Zelda

I like the art style of this series and found a sprite sheet similar this this and used it.

The outside area is bright and green.

Inside the dungeon is quite dark and it gives the player an effect of the unknown beyond the darkness.

I used a open a chest sound effect that was based from this series.


Dragon Quest

I liked the screen this game series uses for battles.

I created my battle screen based on this.

The main character from my animation uses a similar holding sword pose which I based from.


Forager

I liked the bright colors of the ouside area in this game.

This game has nice sprites.

Uses interesting sound effects.


Earthbound

This game has a battle screen similar to Dragon Quest although Earthbound has intresting effects on the backgrounds.


Fairune

The art style for this game was quite nice.

It is quite similar to the Zelda style.
